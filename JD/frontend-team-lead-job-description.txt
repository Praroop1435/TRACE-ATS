Frontend Team Lead job description - Requirements and Skills
==================================================
- Experience in providing technical guidance to a team.
- Highly proficient with JavaScript frameworks.
- Solid understanding of HTML5, CSS3/4, and modern web design principles.
- Passion for coding interactive user experiences.
- Comfortable with Git.
- Bonus: Experience in team building and leadership.
- Bonus: Familiarity with NodeJS, Ruby on Rails, SEO principles, web accessibility.
- Bonus: Experience with remote working.
