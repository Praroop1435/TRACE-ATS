Job Title: Junior Data Analyst – Entry Level

Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and analyze structured data.
- Prepare dashboards using Excel/Power BI/Tableau.
- Write SQL queries for data extraction.
- Support senior analysts in generating business insights.

Required Skills:
- Excel, SQL, Python (Pandas, Numpy)
- Data visualization basics (Matplotlib/Seaborn/Tableau)
- Attention to detail, problem-solving mindset

Preferred Skills:
- Exposure to statistics & hypothesis testing
- Knowledge of cloud tools (AWS/GCP)

Education:
- Bachelor’s in IT, CSE, Statistics, or related fields
