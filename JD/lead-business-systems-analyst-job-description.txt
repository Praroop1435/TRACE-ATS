Lead Business Systems Analyst job description - Requirements and Skills
==================================================
- BSc in Computer Science, Engineering, or a relevant field
- 4-12 years of work experience in software design, development, or implementation
- Project management experience
- Familiarity with cloud-based business software is a plus
- Experience working across departments as a Business Analyst or Consultant
- Analytical and problem-solving skills
- Strong communication skills and ability to influence diverse stakeholders
- Positive, can-do attitude
